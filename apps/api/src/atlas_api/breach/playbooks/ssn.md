# Leaked Social Security number / national ID

**Priority: CRITICAL — enables new-account fraud and tax fraud.**

## Do this now
- **Freeze your credit at all three bureaus** — Equifax, Experian, TransUnion. Freezes are free, don't affect your score, and are the single strongest block on new-account fraud. [1][2]
- Place a free 1-year fraud alert (one call covers all three bureaus). [1]

## Do this soon
- Request an IRS Identity Protection PIN (IP PIN) to block tax-return fraud. [3]
- Report the stolen number to the SSA and ask about a Direct Deposit Fraud Prevention block. [4]
- Pull your free credit reports and review for accounts you didn't open. [5]
- Build a personalized recovery plan and file an FTC Identity Theft Report (supports disputes and police reports). [6]

## Keep doing
- Monitor credit reports and statements for months — this is a marathon, not a one-time fix.
