# Leaked bank or credit-card information

**Priority: HIGH — act fast; fraud protections are time-limited.**
*General information, not financial advice — contact your bank directly.*

## Do this now
- Call the bank or card issuer using the number on the back of your card; flag the account and have the card reissued. [1]
- Review recent transactions and dispute anything unfamiliar promptly. [1]
- Change your online-banking password, enable MFA, and turn on transaction alerts.

## Do this soon
- Consider a credit freeze if account-opening fraud is a risk. [2]

## Keep doing
- Watch statements and credit reports for several months. [3]
