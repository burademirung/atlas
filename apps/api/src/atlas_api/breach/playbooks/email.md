# Compromised email account

**Priority: HIGH — your email resets every other account, so secure it first.**

## Do this now
- Regain control: reset the password and sign out all sessions. [1]
- Turn on MFA. [1]
- Remove anything an attacker may have added: forwarding rules, filters, connected apps, and recovery email/phone. [1]

## Do this soon
- Rotate passwords on any account that uses this email for password resets. [2]
- Warn your contacts if the account may have sent phishing.

## Keep doing
- Review account activity and connected apps periodically.
