# Leaked medical / health records

**Priority: MEDIUM-HIGH — medical identity theft is harder to detect and undo.**

## Do this now
- Contact your providers, pharmacy, and health insurer; ask for your records and a list of benefits paid in your name. Look for care you never received. [1]

## Do this soon
- Watch for unfamiliar medical bills or debt-collection notices. [1]
- If your health information was mishandled, you can file a complaint with HHS Office for Civil Rights (HIPAA). [2]
- File an FTC Identity Theft Report if your identity was used. [3]

## Keep doing
- Correct errors in your medical records in writing and keep copies.
