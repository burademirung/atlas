How a question becomes a cited answer.

Atlas reads the live web, reasons over what it finds, and writes back a sourced report — in seconds. Here is exactly what happens in between, one moving part at a time.

First, the cast

Six parts, in plain language

🧭

Your browser

The person at the counter who places the order.

Sends your question, then shows the answer as it streams in.

🛰️

Cloudflare Worker

A receptionist with a desk in 300+ cities at once.

Code at the network edge, nearest to you. Routes the request.

🧠

Claude Opus 4.8

The researcher who actually reads and reasons.

The AI model. Decides what to look up, then writes the report.

🔎

Web search tool

The researcher's library card.

Lets Claude look things up live and bring back real sources.

📡

Live stream (SSE)

A radio broadcast — you hear it forming, word by word.

Server-Sent Events push each update instead of making you wait.

🗄️

D1 database

The filing cabinet where finished reports are kept.

Cloudflare's edge SQL store. Every run is saved for later.

01

You ask a question.

You type something like “What are the newest EV battery chemistries?” and hit run. Your browser packages it up and sends it off.

Browser → POST /api/research

02

The nearest city catches it.

The request lands at a Cloudflare Worker in the data center closest to you — one of 300+ worldwide. No far-away server to wait on.

Cloudflare edge · runs in milliseconds, near the user

03

The Worker briefs the researcher.

The Worker hands your question to Claude along with instructions: research this, cite your sources, write it clearly.

Worker → Anthropic API · claude-opus-4-8

04

Claude decides to look it up.

Instead of guessing from memory, Claude chooses to search — like deciding to open a browser and check. It writes its own search queries.

Native web_search tool · up to 5 searches

05

It reads the live web.

Real pages come back — official docs, news, references. Claude pulls the relevant passages and tracks where each fact came from.

Live results · each becomes a numbered citation

06

The answer streams back.

Every word flows back the moment it's written — Claude → Worker → your browser — like a live broadcast, not a long wait.

Server-Sent Events (SSE) · token-by-token

07

Your screen fills in, live.

You watch the pipeline light up and the cited report assemble itself — sources in the margin, footnotes linking back to them.

Rendered safely · model output escaped, then formatted

08

It's saved for next time.

The finished report and its sources drop into the filing cabinet, so you can reopen any past run from your history.
